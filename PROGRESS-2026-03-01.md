### 18:01 - Fix form differentiation (session's critical fix)
Deep analysis comparing HTML reference line-by-line against Metal physics revealed 2 compounding math bugs that erased Chladni mode structure:

**Bug 1: Friction 53x too weak**
- HTML: `baseFric = pow(0.06, dt)` = 0.954/frame (4.6% velocity loss)
- Metal: `pow(u.damping, dt)` where damping=0.95 = 0.999/frame (0.086% loss)
- All modes hit the speed cap uniformly, erasing pattern differentiation
- Fix: `dynamicFric = baseFric * nodeBrake` (use base friction, not UI slider)

**Bug 2: Integration 60x too slow**
- HTML: `p.x += p.vx * dt * 60` (the `* 60` converts per-frame velocity units)
- Metal: `px += vx * u.dt` (missing `* 60`)
- Force weight was retuned (0.45 to 27.0) but integration wasn't updated to match
- Fix: `px += vx * u.dt * 60.0f`

Also reduced Heisenberg uncertainty noise coefficient from 12 to 3 for cleaner form settling while keeping the quantum physics intact.
